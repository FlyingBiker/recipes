Ingredients:
 - 1 1/4 cups sugar
 - 1/2 cup chopped pecans
 - 1 tsp ground cinnamon
 - 1 tsp butter, softened
 - 2 large eggs
 - 1 cup mashed bananas (2 bananas)
 - 1/2 sour cream
 - 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
 - 2 cups flour
 - 1 tsp Baking Powder
 - 1 tsp Baking Soda
 - 1/4 tsp Salt

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease bundt pan.
 
 2. Stir together 1/4 cup sugar, pecans, and cinnamon.  Sprinkle 1/2 of
    mixture in well-greased bundt pan.  Beat butter at medium speed until creamy.  Gradually add remaining cup suger, beat 5 - 7 minutes until light and fluffy.  Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating just until yellow disappears.  Add banana, sour cream and vanilla.  Beat at low speed just until blended. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Fold into butter mixture.  Pour 1/2 batter into prepared pan.  Sprinkle remaining pecan mixture.  Top with remining batter
 
 3. Bake 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
